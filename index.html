About Us

Who We Are

Launched on the 18th of November 2019. Plus You belives that style has no size limit. Our brand is built around a simple yet powerful philosophy-fashion should be for everyone, without compromise. We are dedicated to providing plus-size women with clothing that embraces their unique beauty while offering elegance, comfort, and affordability.

Plus You is more than just a clothing store;it's a celebration of confidence, self expression, and body positivity. Our team is passionate about curating a collection that caters to all your needs, from everyday essentials to statement pieces for those special moments.We take pride in understanding the diverse needs of our community and ensuring that each piece reflects our committment to quality and style.

This is also reflected in our plus-size beauty pageant held every two years.

Our Mission

To dress and empower the beautiful plus-size women through trendy, exclusive and quality fashions.

Our mission is to empower plus-size women to feel confident and beautiful in their own skin, wherever life takes them. We believe in delivering more than just clothing; we offer a lifestyle that celebrates you-fun-loving, adventurous, and unapologetically yourself. At Plus You, we understand that you deserve clothes that move with you, fit like a dream, and make you feel like your best self.

Join the Plus You Family Today!

Step into the world where fashion knows no boundaries. Whether yo're exploring new cities, enjoying a weekend gate away, or simply looking for that perfect piece to complete your wardrope.

Our Vision

To be a premium and reliable provider of quality plus-size fashion.

At Plus You we strive to redefine plus-size by offering clothing that inspires confidence, embraces individuality, and celebrates the beauty of every woman. We are committed to creating stylish, elegant, and comfortable pieces that cater to the unique needs of plus-size women, providing that great style knows no size.

Plus You is dedicated to delivering high quality fashion at prices that don't break the bank. We are passionate about bringing a little luxury to your wardrope, without sacrificing comfort or fit. With every piece we create, our goal is to help you build a closet that you love and that makes you feel unstoppable.

Empowering Style, Celebrating You Always.

Products Provided

Designer Dresses

We have high fashion garments with exceptional quality, unique styles and attention to detail featuring luxurious fabrics, intricate embellishments, and cutting edge designs making them a statement of elegance, sophistication and exclusivity crafted by renowned designers..

Lingerie

Explore a luxurious collection of lingerie designed for comfort, elegance, and confidence.From delicate lace to smooth satin, our pieces offer the perfect blend of style and sensuality for every occassion. Find your perfect fit and feel amazing!

Denim Wear

Shop our collection of stylish denim wear featuring a variety of jeans, jackets,and skirts for every occassion. These are made from quality material and offer comfort, durability and timeless fashion. Perfect for casual outings or adding a chic edge to your wardrope..

Foot Wear

Discover our wide selection of stylish and comfortable footwear for every occassion. From casual sneakers to elegant dress shoes, each pair is crafted with high quality material for durability and comfort. Find your perfect fit today!

Jewelery

Discover a stunning collection of exclusive jewelery featuring unique designs crafted with precision and care. From elegant necklaces and sparkling bracelets to intricate watches and statement earings each piece made with high quality material. Shop now and add touch of luxury to your collection.

Designer Bags

Luxury handbags are not just functional items at Plus You but also pieces of art that are crafted using high-quality materials, intricate designs, and exquisite detailing. These handbags are often produced in limited quantities, adding to their exclusivity and increasing their value over time.

Cosmetics

Explore our wide range of high-quality cosmetics, featuring skincare, makeup, and beauty essentials. From foundations and lipsticks to moisturisers and serum, we offer products that cater to all skin types and beauty needs, ensuring a flawless look and radiant skin.
